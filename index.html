About Me

I am a Senior Software Engineer who wrote his first program at the age of 12 - it was a simple slot machine that was created as I read Teach Yourself C in 24 Hours. I had a lot of fun but I wasn't hooked - at least not yet. It wasn't until I started playing an online game, many years later, that was written in PHP that I was hooked. I was fascinated with how the inner workings of the game operated and I began to teach myself PHP. Eventually I joined the game's staff volunteering my time to help fix defects, make small updates and further my knowledge of PHP and MySQL.
